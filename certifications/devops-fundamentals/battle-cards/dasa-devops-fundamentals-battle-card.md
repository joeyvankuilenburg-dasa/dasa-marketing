# DASA DevOps Fundamentals Certification Program

## Product Overview

The DASA DevOps Fundamentals Certification Program builds the essential knowledge, skills, and mindset to successfully participate in DevOps ways of working. It helps individuals understand how to foster collaboration, drive efficiency, and spark innovation, laying the groundwork for DevOps success at scale.

### Who it's for

- Team members who contribute to software delivery (e.g., developers, operations engineers, testers, architects)
- IT professionals in service management or support roles
- Business and product leaders seeking to align their teams for faster, value-driven results
- Change agents involved in enabling DevOps adoption across teams and departments

### Elevator Pitch

The DASA DevOps Fundamentals Certification equips your teams with a shared understanding of DevOps principles, practices, and culture. Build the foundation for collaboration, automation, measurement, and continuous learning, so your organization can deliver faster, reduce waste, and maximize value.

## Key Differentiators

- **Backed by the world's largest DevOps industry body**: Developed by DASA, not tied to any vendor or toolset.
- **Emphasizes culture and collaboration as core pillars**: Goes beyond tools and automation by putting culture and shared responsibility at the heart of DevOps success.
- **Built on the proven CALMS model and DASA's six DevOps principles**: Provides a structured, principle-based approach that connects mindset, methods, and measurable outcomes.
- **Integrates measurement and success metrics from day one**: Helps teams define and track the metrics that matter.
