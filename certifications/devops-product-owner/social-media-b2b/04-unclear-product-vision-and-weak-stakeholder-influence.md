When the product vision isn't clear, every stakeholder pulls in a different direction, and Product Owners lose the influence to steer decisions. A strong vision should unite people, not divide them.

The DASA DevOps Product Owner Certification empowers you to define, communicate, and defend a compelling product vision.
→ Learn vision-setting and prioritization techniques rooted in Agile and DevOps
→ Engage and influence stakeholders around measurable business outcomes
→ Gain the confidence to lead conversations with strategy, not just stories

Because Product Owners don't just build products, they build alignment.
