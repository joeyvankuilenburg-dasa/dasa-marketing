When business, dev, and ops don't speak the same language, Product Owners end up stuck in the middle, translating priorities no one fully agrees on. Building great products should be a team sport, not a tug-of-war between departments.

The DASA DevOps Product Owner Certification helps bridge business and IT through a shared value focus.
→ Understand how Agile, Lean, and DevOps combine to drive collaboration
→ Learn to translate strategic intent into actionable backlog items
→ Create alignment across teams using a proven framework

When everyone understands the "why," alignment becomes natural, and delivery becomes unstoppable.
