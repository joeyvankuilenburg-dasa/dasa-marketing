# DASA DevOps Coach Certification Program

## Product Overview

The DASA DevOps Coach Certification Program is an immersive 2-day training that empowers professionals with the mindset, skills, and techniques to become powerful change agents in high-performance digital organizations.

### Who it's for

- Agile, DevOps, and transformation coaches
- Scrum masters, team leads, agile practitioners
- Change agents, organizational development professionals
- Leaders and managers supporting DevOps and agile transformations

### Elevator Pitch

The DASA DevOps Coach Certification Program helps organizations unlock the full potential of their teams by building a strong coaching capability across the enterprise. Certified DevOps Coaches play a pivotal role in embedding new ways of working, accelerating change adoption, and creating a culture of continuous improvement.

## Key Enterprise Pain Points

| Enterprise Challenge | How the Certification Program Helps |
|---|---|
| Resistance to change during transformation initiatives | Equips coaches to support individuals and teams through mindset shifts and behavioral change |
| Lack of team ownership and engagement | Builds coaching skills that empower teams and foster psychological safety |
| Ineffective communication and collaboration across silos | Teaches coaching interventions that bridge gaps and improve cross-functional interaction |
| Agile and DevOps practices failing to scale or stick | Enables coaches to reinforce habits, support continuous improvement, and sustain momentum |
| Lack of clarity and focus in leadership support | Prepares coaches to work with leaders on building alignment and reinforcing DevOps culture |

## Key Differentiators

- **Coach the System, Not Just the Team**: Goes beyond team-level coaching to include leadership, systems thinking, and organizational culture.
- **Built on DevOps Principles**: Grounded in the DASA DevOps Principles that promote autonomy, purpose, mastery, and cross-functional collaboration.
- **Balance Between Hard and Soft Skills**: Blends practical coaching techniques with emotional intelligence, feedback loops, and communication frameworks.
- **Cultural Enablement at the Core**: Designed to embed learning, experimentation, and psychological safety into the heart of the organization.
