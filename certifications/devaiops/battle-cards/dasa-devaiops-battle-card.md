# DASA DevAIOps Certification Program

## Product Overview

The DASA DevAIOps Certification develops AI-driven automation, optimization, and decision-making skills, enabling you to enhance software development, IT operations, and business agility in an AI-powered world, achieving faster time-to-market and time-to-value.

### Who it's for

- Those driving AI adoption in DevOps (e.g., IT Directors, AIOps Architects, DevOps Engineers)
- Teams improving operational efficiency and product delivery (e.g., Developers, Software Managers, Product Owners)
- Roles responsible for security, compliance, and ethical AI (e.g., IT Security Analysts, Business Managers)
- Professionals leading continuous improvement and innovation (e.g., Cloud Engineers, IT Operations Staff, Scrum Masters)

### Elevator Pitch

DASA DevAIOps helps enterprise IT and transformation teams who want to confidently integrate AI into their DevOps practices by equipping them with the literacy, governance, innovation, and data mastery needed to deliver faster, smarter, and more secure digital products.
