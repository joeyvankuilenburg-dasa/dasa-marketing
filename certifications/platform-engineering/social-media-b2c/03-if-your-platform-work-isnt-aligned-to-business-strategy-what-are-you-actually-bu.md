Too many platform teams deliver great work that never connects back to business goals. The result? Effort without recognition or measurable value.

The DASA Platform Engineering Certification helps you change that. You'll gain the tools to align every platform decision with strategy, ROI, and real outcomes.

→ Link platform work directly to business objectives
→ Measure and communicate impact clearly
→ Drive value, not just velocity

Because platform work isn't about output, it's about impact.
