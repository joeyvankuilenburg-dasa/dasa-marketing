Too many platforms are not built for the users. The real success of a platform isn't how it's made, but how it's adopted.

DASA teaches platform engineering as a product discipline, with adoption and developer experience at its core. Because platforms should be designed for the people who use them, not just the people who build them.

→ Treat the platform as a product
→ Focus on developer experience to drive real adoption
→ Build what your users actually need

Because the best engineers don't just build platforms, they build products people believe in.
