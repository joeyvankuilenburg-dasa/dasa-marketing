This is where most platforms fail, not because the tech is wrong, but because the platform was never treated as a product. Without developer-centric design and intentional adoption strategies, even the best-built platform collects dust.

The DASA Platform Engineering Certification helps teams:

→ Treat the platform as a product
→ Focus on developer experience for real adoption

Because even the best-built platform is worthless if it doesn't serve its users.
