Too many platform teams get stuck delivering features that have no clear tie to business goals. It's not that the work is bad, it's that it's disconnected.

The DASA Platform Engineering Certification provides actionable tools and approaches to tightly align platform initiatives with business strategy and ROI.

→ Link platform work directly to business objectives
→ Measure and communicate impact clearly
→ Drive value, not just velocity

Because platform work should fuel business results, not drift aimlessly in its own silo.
