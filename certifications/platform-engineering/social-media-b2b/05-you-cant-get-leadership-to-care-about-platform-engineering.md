You know your platform work matters. But leadership sees it as plumbing, necessary, but not strategic. And that makes getting sponsorship, resources, and momentum feel impossible.

This isn't just about funding. It's about leadership understanding the role of platform engineering in driving business success.

The DASA Platform Engineering Certification empowers:

→ Leaders to advocate for and fund platform initiatives
→ Teams to align platform work with business strategy
→ Organizations to scale platforms with the right cultural and leadership support

Because great platforms need champions at every level, especially at the top.
