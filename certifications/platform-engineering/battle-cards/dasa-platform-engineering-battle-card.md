# DASA Platform Engineering Certification Program

## Product Overview

The DASA Platform Engineering Certification Program is a 2-day training that empowers professionals to design, implement, and scale high-performance developer platforms. It covers key concepts such as platform-as-a-product, developer experience, infrastructure as code, CI/CD, and platform governance.

### Who it's for

- Platform Teams & Engineers (i.e. platform engineers, SREs, DevOps engineers)
- Technology Leadership (i.e. CTOs, Heads of Engineering, IT leaders)
- Product Teams (i.e. Product managers, Platform product owners)
- Transformation Leaders (i.e. Enterprise architects, DevOps coaches, Agile consultants)

### Elevator Pitch

The DASA Platform Engineering Certification equips your teams to build developer platforms that deliver business value. Instead of just deploying technology, it teaches how to align platform efforts with strategic objectives, improve developer experience, and embed sustainable practices for platform success.

## Key Enterprise Pain Points

| Enterprise Challenge | How the Certification Program Helps |
|---|---|
| Platforms that don't get adopted or used effectively | Teaches how to treat the platform as a product and focus on developer experience |
| Misalignment between business strategy and platform work | Provides tools and thinking to align platform engineering with strategic business goals |
| Cultural resistance and siloed ownership | Equips teams with strategies for stakeholder management, collaboration, and cultural alignment |
| Disjointed tooling and inconsistent platform outcomes | Provides a structured approach covering IaC, CI/CD, monitoring, and containerization |
| Lack of leadership understanding and sponsorship | Trains leaders to advocate for and support platform initiatives from the top-down |

## Key Differentiators

- **Think Beyond the Build**: Treat the platform as a product and foster adoption.
- **End-to-End Framework**: Covers the full platform lifecycle, from planning and design to scaling and governance.
- **Business Alignment**: Provides the tools to align platform initiatives with organizational strategy and ROI.
- **Leadership Enablement**: Offers structured learning for leaders to effectively support and fund platform initiatives.
