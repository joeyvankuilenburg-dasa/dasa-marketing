# DASA Transformational Leader Certification Program

## Product Overview

DASA Transformational Leader equips transformation leaders with the skills, mindset, and practical tools to lead enterprise DevOps journeys at scale. Too often, DevOps efforts stall because they lack strategic alignment, leadership buy-in, and a clear operating model.

### Who it's for

- Enterprise and transformation leaders driving organization-wide change
- DevOps coaches and consultants building transformation playbooks
- CIOs, CTOs, and heads of engineering shaping digital strategy
- Managers responsible for building high-performing product teams

### Elevator Pitch

The DASA Transformational Leader Certification empowers your organization to lead DevOps transformations that actually stick. By developing leaders who understand how to align vision, culture, governance, and technology, you'll build the conditions for autonomy, flow, and high performance.

## Key Enterprise Pain Points

| Enterprise Challenge | How the Certification Program Helps |
|---|---|
| DevOps efforts stall due to lack of leadership or strategic alignment | Teaches leaders how to craft a compelling transformation story and align teams across silos |
| Siloed structures and conflicting incentives undermine transformation | Focuses on breaking down organizational silos and building shared goals |
| Resistance to change and cultural inertia | Equips leaders with proven change management and leadership techniques |
| Lack of practical guidance to scale DevOps | Provides actionable blueprints based on DASA's transformation model |
| Difficulty measuring transformation progress | Teaches leaders to track progress using the DASA Team Scorecard |
| Transformation efforts lack momentum or clarity | Enables leaders to define the 'why,' shape the journey, and measure progress |
| IT and business remain misaligned on value creation | Guides leaders on creating flow, reducing barriers, and designing architectures that enable responsiveness |

## Key Differentiators

- **Transformation, not just training**: Based on real-world research into DevOps transformations, with a structured toolkit.
- **Performance infrastructure, not just practices**: Learn how to create conditions for flow, autonomy, and trust.
- **Leadership in action**: Includes a live transformation plan assessed by seasoned leaders.
- **Strategic alignment**: Helps leaders connect transformation efforts with business objectives.
- **Peer-driven transformation insights**: Designed as a peer-learning journey.
- **Built-in reflection and growth**: Rooted in experiential learning, helping participants adapt leadership styles.
