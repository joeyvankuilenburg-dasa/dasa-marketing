Keeping systems reliable shouldn't come at the expense of sustainability. Yet too often, high performance means high waste: draining energy, resources, and budgets.

DASA SRE Next Gen brings green SRE practices to the forefront:
→ Reduce operational waste and energy consumption
→ Design architectures that balance resilience with environmental responsibility
→ Equip your team to build high-performing, sustainable systems

Because reliable systems shouldn't just work, they should work responsibly.
