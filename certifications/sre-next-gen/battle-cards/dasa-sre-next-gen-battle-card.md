# DASA SRE Next Gen Certification Program

## Product Overview

DASA SRE Next Gen helps enterprise teams modernize their approach to reliability in an era of AI, automation, and distributed systems. Systems are more fragmented than ever. AI, self-healing infrastructure, and autonomous operations have changed the game, but traditional SRE methods weren't designed for this. They haven't kept up with the scale, speed, or complexity of modern architectures, leading to costly outages, inefficiencies, and misalignment with business goals. This certification fills that gap by building the skills, mindset, and execution capability needed to deliver resilience, reduce risk, and support smarter growth.

### Who it's for

- Those evolving SRE capabilities across product lines (e.g., platform teams, reliability leads, and engineering directors)
- Teams delivering digital experiences at scale (e.g., DevOps engineers, developers, site reliability engineers, cloud architects)
- Leaders aligning tech investments with business and sustainability goals (e.g., CTOs, heads of engineering, transformation leaders)
- Practitioners modernizing observability, incident response, and automation
- Change agents responsible for scaling reliability culture and governance

### Elevator Pitch

The DASA SRE Next Gen Certification gives you the skills, mindset, and behaviors to align reliability practices with business goals, leverage AI and automation, and optimize system resilience and scalability, while ensuring sustainability.
