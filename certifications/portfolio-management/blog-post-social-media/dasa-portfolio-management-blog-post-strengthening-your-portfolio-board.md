# DASA Portfolio Management - Blog Post Social: Strengthening Your Portfolio Board

Why do some portfolio boards drive progress while others create friction?

The difference is rarely process alone. It comes down to behaviors, decision-making, and the environment leaders create around priorities, funding, and accountability.

In this latest blog, we explore how to strengthen your portfolio board by encouraging the behaviors that lead to better outcomes. Learn practical best practices to improve alignment, speed up decisions, and create more value across the organization.
