# DASA Portfolio Management Certification Program

## Product Overview

The DASA Portfolio Management Certification Program is a 2-day training that equips professionals with the skills, mindset, and tools to align portfolios with organizational goals and drive sustainable business value.

### Who it's for

- Portfolio managers, product managers, epic owners
- Transformation leaders, product owners, enterprise architects
- Project managers, scrum masters, business leaders involved in strategic planning and execution

### Elevator Pitch

The DASA Portfolio Management Certification Program enables you to move from reactive planning to proactive, value-driven portfolio governance. Learn how to align initiatives with strategic goals, optimize for impact, and lead with data-backed decisions.

## Key Enterprise Pain Points

| Enterprise Challenge | How the Certification Program Helps |
|---|---|
| Lack of alignment between initiatives and strategic goals | Teaches how to define a portfolio vision and strategy tied to business objectives |
| Fragmented decision-making and low transparency | Provides governance models and data-driven practices for effective oversight |
| Wasted resources on low-impact projects | Offers tools for prioritization, value stream alignment, and funding optimization |
| Difficulty scaling agile practices to strategic planning | Connects value streams to strategic goals with a full portfolio lifecycle framework |
| Lack of ownership and accountability in portfolio execution | Builds stakeholder alignment and defines governance roles |

## Key Differentiators

- **Full Lifecycle Governance**: Covers strategy formulation, execution alignment, governance, and continuous improvement.
- **Value Stream Alignment**: Helps structure portfolios around value streams for maximum business impact.
- **Strategic Resource Optimization**: Ensures resources are allocated based on strategic alignment and potential impact.
- **Insight-Led Decision Making**: Equips teams with governance models and metrics for transparent, data-driven decisions.
- **Priority on What Matters Most**: Enables you to identify and act on initiatives that deliver the greatest strategic value.
- **Risk Resilience Built-In**: Embeds proactive risk identification and mitigation across portfolio planning and execution.
