- Optimize decision-making with governance models and actionable metrics

Because it shouldn't be this difficult to focus on what truly drives business success.

The DASA Portfolio Management Certification Program equips you with insight-led governance and decision-making practices:

### Wasted resources on low-impact projects

Your teams are busy, but the results don't reflect the effort. When resources get spread thin across low-impact projects, work becomes motion without progress.
