When your portfolio is organized around departments instead of value streams, work often serves the org chart, not the customer.
→ Focus on continuous value delivery across the portfolio
→ Structure work around customer outcomes, not internal silos
→ Make prioritization and funding decisions based on value, not politics

Learn at your own pace and take the next step in your career with confidence.
