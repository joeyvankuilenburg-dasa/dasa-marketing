If your security approach is based on reacting to yesterday's threats, it's already out of date.

Attackers are adapting fast. Your defenses should be too.

The DASA Intelligent Continuous Security™ Certification teaches your teams to
→ adopt proactive and adaptive security measures
→ shift from reactive firefighting to continuous, intelligent protection
→ stay ahead of threats by integrating security into the flow of work
