Teams are busy. Work is happening. But is it moving you closer to your strategic goals?

Too often, product teams are left guessing whether their work supports the bigger picture.

The DASA Product Management Certification Program helps you shift from project to product.

You'll build leadership capability across the entire product team and learn how to define a product vision that truly aligns with business strategy.

Here's what makes this program different:
→ From project to product: Focus on value, not just delivery
→ Full lifecycle coverage: Master strategy, design, operations, and evolution
→ Customer-first product thinking: Apply market discovery, UX/CX alignment, and value measurement
→ Transformation enabler: Align stakeholders and fuel enterprise-wide change
→ Capability over role: Build product leadership across contributors, leaders, and enablers
→ Methodology-inclusive: Succeed in any context, regardless of how your organization works

Because every product deserves a clear purpose, not just activity for the sake of activity.
