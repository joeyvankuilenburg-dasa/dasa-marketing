Misunderstood roles lead to dropped balls, double work, and frustration across teams. When responsibilities aren't clear, collaboration falls apart.

The DASA Product Management Certification Program clarifies roles, responsibilities, and collaboration models, developing product management capabilities across the entire product team, not just a single role.

Because success comes when everyone understands their role in making the product succeed.
