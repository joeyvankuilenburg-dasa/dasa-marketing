# DASA Product Management Certification Program

## Product Overview

The DASA Product Management Certification Program is a 2-day training designed to build exceptional product management capabilities across roles. It equips professionals with the skills, mindset, and behaviors to lead product strategy, streamline product operations, and drive customer-centric innovation.

### Who it's for

- Product contributors: Product managers, product owners, UX designers, business analysts
- Stakeholders & enablers: Agile and DevOps coaches, transformation leads, business owners, scrum masters, tech leads

### Elevator Pitch

The DASA Product Management Certification Program helps you move beyond Agile delivery to master product leadership. It empowers teams to adopt a product-centric mindset, align product work with strategic goals, and deliver greater business value by managing the entire product lifecycle with confidence.

## Key Enterprise Pain Points

| Enterprise Challenge | How the Certification Program Helps |
|---|---|
| Lack of product vision and alignment with business strategy | Teaches how to define a compelling product vision and align it with organizational goals |
| Slow new product delivery and delayed time-to-market | Provides structured planning, roadmap development, and delivery strategies |
| Product decisions made without market or user insight | Equips teams with discovery techniques and customer-centric product planning |
| Fragmented product delivery processes | Offers a full lifecycle framework, including operations, planning, and continuous value delivery |
| Misunderstanding of product roles across functions | Clarifies roles, responsibilities, and collaboration models |
| Failure to embed product thinking across the org | Builds the product-centric mindset across contributors, leaders, and stakeholders |

## Key Differentiators

- **From Project to Product**: Shifts teams from delivery-focused to value-focused by embedding product leadership principles.
- **Full Lifecycle Coverage**: Teaches product management across strategy, design, operations, and evolution.
- **Customer-First Product Thinking**: Drives better outcomes through market discovery, UX/CX alignment, and value measurement.
- **Transformation Enabler**: Helps align stakeholders and product teams, fueling larger enterprise change.
- **Capability Over Role**: Develop product management capabilities across the entire product team.
- **Methodology-Inclusive**: Success in any context, regardless of how your organization works.
