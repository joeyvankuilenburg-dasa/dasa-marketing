# DASA Experience Management for HPDO Certification Program

## Product Overview

The DASA Experience Management Certification equips teams to deliver consistent, engaging, and impactful digital experiences. It teaches your people how to embed experience management into strategy, delivery, and daily practice, helping you win and retain users in an increasingly experience-driven world.

### Who it's for

- Leadership & Strategy (i.e. Leadership teams, Finance)
- Product & Service Teams (i.e. Product owners/leaders, Service managers, Product developers)
- Experience & Design (i.e. CX professionals, UX designers)
- Agile & IT Enablement (i.e. Agile & DevOps coaches, IT professionals)

### Elevator Pitch

The DASA Experience Management Certification equips teams to deliver consistent, engaging, and impactful digital experiences. It teaches your people how to embed experience management into strategy, delivery, and daily practice.

## Key Enterprise Pain Points

| Enterprise Challenge | How the Certification Program Helps |
|---|---|
| Disconnected experiences lead to churn, low adoption, and missed opportunities | Teaches holistic experience design and delivery that aligns user needs with business goals |
| Fragmented teams and siloed processes create inconsistent journeys | Provides tools and practices to break down silos and establish shared accountability |
| Lack of actionable insights into user behaviors and expectations | Teaches experience mapping, persona development, and data-driven decision-making |
| Resistance to change and misaligned culture | Cultivates behaviors and mindset shifts that reinforce user-centric thinking |
| Traditional metrics fail to capture experience quality | Introduces Key Proudness Indicators (KPIs) to foster intrinsic motivation and measure what really matters |

## Key Differentiators

- **Enhance User Satisfaction**: Deliver seamless experiences that exceed user expectations and boost engagement.
- **Boost Stakeholder Engagement**: Align and empower teams with real-time insights and clear feedback loops.
- **Proactive Issue Resolution**: Anticipate and address experience bottlenecks before they escalate.
- **Propel Organizational Success**: Create a seamless alignment between user needs, stakeholder goals, and organizational strategy.

## Alternatives

| Certification | Key Focus Areas | DASA Differentiation |
|---|---|---|
| CXPA | Customer experience strategy and metrics | DASA expands beyond customer to include employees and partners |
| Nielsen Norman UX Certification | Human-centered design and UX research | DASA takes a broader organizational and behavioral view |
| Design Thinking Certifications (IDEO, LUMA) | Innovation, ideation, creative problem solving | DASA emphasizes embedding experience management into ongoing operations |
| ITIL 4 Drive Stakeholder Value | Service experience in IT context | DASA has wider scope including cultural, behavioral, and product experience |
