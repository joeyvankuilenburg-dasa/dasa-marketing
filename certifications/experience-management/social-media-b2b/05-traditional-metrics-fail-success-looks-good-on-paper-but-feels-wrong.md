The numbers look fine on paper, but something's missing. Your metrics don't tell the real story, and your team is left wondering if their work actually makes a difference.

The DASA Experience Management Certification introduces Key Proudness Indicators (KPIs): metrics that measure what really matters to users and to your teams.

→ Enhance user satisfaction by focusing on outcomes that resonate, not just outputs
→ Boost stakeholder engagement through meaningful metrics that create ownership and pride
→ Proactively improve experiences by measuring the things that drive loyalty, not just efficiency

Because success isn't just about hitting targets. It's about knowing you made something worth being proud of.
